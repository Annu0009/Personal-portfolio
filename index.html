About Me

Chhavi Mittal started her journey as a business woman in the year 2015, with meagre capital and one support staff but with a heart full of dreams and a head full of conviction and determination to succeed. After completing bachelors’ degree in Footwear Design and Development, Chhavi set up a small workshop for designer leather bags and accessories to give wings to her creativity. Within a span of a year Chhavi created her own brand by the name “The Leather Warehouse (TLWH)”.
